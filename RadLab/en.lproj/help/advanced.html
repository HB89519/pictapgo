Tips and Tricks

Now you've got the hang of things - it's time to shift into high gear.

Advanced Editing

Tap and hold on the preview image to compare before and after. Tap on the left side for the original. Tap the right to see before the current filter.

Use the left and right arrows on either side of the strength slider to undo / redo a filter.

While browsing filters, tap the status bar at the top of the screen to scroll to the top.

Managing Filters and Recipes

Tap and hold on a thumbnail in the Filter tab to rearrange the filter order.

Tap and hold on a recipe's thumbnail to rename, delete, or share it.

PicTapGo remembers your last 3 recipes. Tap and hold on a recent recipe to save it permanently.

My Style is a collection of your frequently used filters.

Sharing Recipes

Tap and hold a recipe's thumbnail and select Copy Recipe To Clipboard.

Paste the recipe code into an email or text to share with other PicTapGo users.

Tap on a pictapgo:// formatted recipe code on your device to load recipes from other users.
